How A Strategy Dev Completes Your Product Stack

Serious Brand Strategy: Tech has been specially created to help tech builders, founders, and teams understand how brand strategy can help them ship faster, sell smarter, and reach the people who need what they've built. In this two-hour workshop, you'll discover what a Strategy Dev brings to a product team, why their thinking catches problems your roadmap can't see, and how embedding brand strategy early changes not just how you talk about your product — but how you build it.

schedule 10am - 11am

The Pocket Te Reo Māori Apps Guide

location_on Hive HQ 120 Victoria Ave, Whanganui

A Māori APPS tech play time for all ages, to help take out some of the mystery of common Māori language and culture situations.

Always wanted to know what to ask about a Māori welcome - and too scared to get it wrong? Been invited to a marae for work but never been before? Attending a Māori funeral at a local home or marae but don't know what to expect? Looking to up your Te Reo Māori phrases but don't know any speakers for a one-on-one? Whetū takes you through her favourite apps she keeps in her kete to help you on your own Māori language journey.

Hosts: Whetu Fala Te Manu Atatū Whanganui Māori Business Network

calendar_today 21 May
schedule 6pm

The Māori AI Future

location_on Hive HQ 120 Victoria Ave, Whanganui

Where are we in our transformational journey as First Nations people from consumers to creators of emerging technology.

How is all the work that has been done to build Māori AI that reflects our values and voices being used? Building on his PHD thesis work with fellow Dr Kelvin Shedlock, completed in 2025, that explored how Kaupapa Māori can shape AI algorithms, a chance to hear on his latest updates on his work and also the international First Nations AI research work he is involved with, Abundant Intelligences.

Hosts: Dr Petera Hudson (Whakatohea), Sponsored by Te Manu Atatū Whanganui Māori Business network.
